Common Errors

"Untrusted Enterprise Developer"?

You will sometimes need to trust our certificate when downloading our apps. To do this:
Open Settings > General > Profiles And Device Management > Click on our certificate > Click Trust.
Sometimes the "Profiles And Device Management" is named differently, but it will still be similar.
